Castle Air Museum

The museum resides near Castle Airport, which operated as Castle Air Force Base during the Cold War and was home to the 93rd Bombardment Wing of Strategic Air Command. Its collection numbers over seventy historical military aircraft, the majority of which are displayed outdoors.

Yanks Air Museum

Established in 1973, Yanks Air Museum is one of two museums located on the grounds of Chino Airport and boasts a collection of nearly two hundred aircraft.

Commemorative Air Force SoCal Wing

The Commemorative Air Force is an organization devoted to restoring, operating, and maintaining historic aircraft. Their inventory of over 100 airworthy warbirds is distributed across a nationwide network of wings. Their Southern California Wing is based in Camarillo, a small town 50 miles east of Los Angeles, on the grounds of Camarillo Airport.
